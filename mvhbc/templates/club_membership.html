Membership

The yearly dues are $20 for individual membership and $25 for family membership. As a member you will receive a free email account, access to the club message board and admission to the competititons, club outings and lots of other fun events.

The Merrimack Homebrewing Club meets the first Saturday of every month. Usually alternating between different pubs, breweries and members who are gracious enough to host the monthly meeting.

If you would like more information please feel contact the club President or you are welcome to attend one of our monthly meetings.
